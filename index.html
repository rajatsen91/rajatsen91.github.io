Biography

I am an Applied Scientist II at Amazon Search. I am a part of team MIDAS lead by Inderjit Dhillon and I am lucky to work with excellent colleagues including Sujay Sanghavi, Arya Mazumdar and Hsiang-Fu Yu. Currently, I am working on various topics including extreme multi-label learning for search and reinforcement learning for search.

I recently obtained my PhD. at UT Austin, where I was advised by Dr. Sanjay Shakkottai. During my time at UT Austin, I have also had the pleasure of working with Dr. Alex Dimakis.

I received my Bachelors degree in ECE, IIT Kharagpur in 2013. I have spent most of my childhood in Durgapur and Kolkata, West Bengal, India.

Over the course of my PhD, I have been lucky to work with excellent collaborators on a variety of problems in the areas of bandit problems [A,B,C], statistical testing [A,B,C], black-box optimization with applications to hyper-parameter tuning [A], queuing systems with learnable parameters [A] and GANs [A].
